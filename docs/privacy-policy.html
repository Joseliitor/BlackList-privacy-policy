Privacy Policy

Blacklist: Debt Tracker  |  TechInn  |  Last updated: April 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how TechInn ("we", "us", or "our") handles information when you use the Blacklist: Debt Tracker Android application (the "App"), version 1.0.0. We are committed to protecting your privacy. Please read this policy carefully.

Core principle: All debt data you enter in Blacklist is stored exclusively on your device. We never collect, transmit, or store your personal financial records on any server.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Data you enter directly

Blacklist stores the following information locally on your device only using an on-device SQLite database (Room):

  • Names of people associated with debts
  • Debt amounts and optional descriptions (concept)
  • Debt type (money you are owed / money you owe)
  • Creation and settlement dates
  • Settled/paid debt history

This data never leaves your device and is never sent to our servers or any third party.

1.2 Contacts (read-only, never uploaded)

The App requests the READ_CONTACTS permission to provide name auto-complete suggestions when you create a new debt entry. This works entirely on-device — your contacts are never uploaded, transmitted, stored outside your device, or shared with anyone.

You may deny the contacts permission; the App remains fully functional — you simply type names manually without auto-complete.

1.3 App preferences

We store small preference flags locally (onboarding completion, Pro upgrade status, total number of debts created) using Android DataStore. This data stays on your device only. Pro status preferences are excluded from Android backups to prevent unauthorised bypass of the upgrade.

1.4 Debt message suggestions ("Ask for it")

The App includes a feature that generates suggested message templates to help you request repayment. These messages are generated entirely on-device using pre-written templates combined with the debt data you have already entered (name, amount, concept). No data is sent to any external service or AI API for this feature.

1.5 Data we do NOT collect

  • No account registration or login required
  • No email address or personal identifiers
  • No location data
  • No analytics or usage telemetry from our side
  • No camera or microphone access
  • No data is ever uploaded to our servers — we operate no backend infrastructure

2. Third-Party Services

2.1 Google AdMob (free version only)

The free version of Blacklist displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob (Google LLC). AdMob may collect and use data to deliver ads, which may include:

  • Device identifiers (advertising ID / GAID)
  • IP address and approximate location
  • App usage signals for ad targeting and measurement

AdMob operates under Google's Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Pro users: After purchasing Blacklist Pro, ads are permanently removed and AdMob is no longer active in the App. No advertising data is collected for Pro users.

EU / EEA users (GDPR): Google AdMob may serve personalised or non-personalised ads. You can opt out of personalised ads at any time via Android Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalisation". A future version of the App will include a GDPR consent screen (Google UMP) to provide explicit consent management in compliance with EU law.

2.2 Google Play Billing

If you purchase the one-time Blacklist Pro upgrade, the transaction is processed exclusively by Google Play. We do not receive or store your payment card details. Purchase validation is performed via Google Play Billing APIs on-device. Google Play's Terms of Service and Privacy Policy apply to this transaction.

On every app launch, the App re-validates your purchase status with Google Play to ensure your Pro entitlement is accurate and up to date.

4. Data Storage & Security

All App data is stored in a local SQLite database on your Android device. The database is accessible only to the Blacklist App and is protected by Android's application sandbox.

App preferences (including Pro status and onboarding state) are stored using Android DataStore and are excluded from device and cloud backups to prevent fraudulent bypass of the Pro upgrade. Your debt records are included in Android's standard backup so you can restore them when changing devices.

Technical safeguards in place:

  • HTTPS-only — cleartext network traffic is disabled in production builds
  • Pro re-validation — purchase status is verified against Google Play on every app launch
  • Backup exclusion — Pro/preferences DataStore file excluded from cloud & device transfer backups
  • Code obfuscation — release builds use R8 minification and resource shrinking
  • No backend servers — we operate no servers that could be compromised to expose your data

5. Data Retention & Deletion

Your debt data is stored on your device for as long as the App is installed. Uninstalling the App permanently deletes all locally stored debt records and preferences. We have no server-side copy of your data — it exists only on your device.

You can delete individual debt records at any time from within the App. All data is permanently erased upon App uninstallation.

6. Children's Privacy

Blacklist is intended for users aged 13 and above and is not directed at children. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child under 13 has provided information through the App, please contact us at the email below and we will take appropriate action.

8. Your Rights

Since all personal data stays on your device, you have full control at all times:

  • Access: You can view all your data within the App at any time.
  • Delete individual records: Use the delete action available in the debt detail screen.
  • Delete all data: Uninstall the App — all data is permanently and irreversibly removed.
  • Revoke contacts permission: Go to Android Settings → Apps → Blacklist → Permissions → Contacts.
  • Opt out of personalised ads: Android Settings → Google → Ads → "Opt out of Ads Personalisation".
  • Restore purchase: Your Pro purchase is tied to your Google Play account and can be restored on any device with the same account.

8.1 EU / EEA Residents (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Union or European Economic Area, you have additional rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR):

  • Right of access — all your data is visible within the App.
  • Right to erasure — uninstalling the App deletes all data permanently.
  • Right to data portability — not applicable as we hold no server-side data.
  • Right to object to processing — for advertising data processed by Google AdMob, opt out via Android Settings or purchase Blacklist Pro to remove ads entirely.
  • Right to lodge a complaint — you may contact your local Data Protection Authority.

The legal basis for processing data through Google AdMob is legitimate interest (free service monetisation) for non-personalised ads, and consent for personalised ads. A dedicated GDPR consent interface (Google UMP) is planned for a future update to ensure full compliance with EU consent requirements.
